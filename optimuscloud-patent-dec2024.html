December 2024

The patent that we had filed on one of the key innovations behind OptimusCloud is granted by the USPTO. This becomes patent number US-12175268-B2.

The co-inventors on the patent are Somali Chaterji (CEO, KeyByte), Saurabh Bagchi (CTO, KeyByte), and Ashraf Mahgoub (Lead Architect, KeyByte).

In this patent, we have the technology that allows a set of servers to be reconfigured without causing downtime to the service. The reconfiguration happens only if the benefit from the reconfiguration exceeds the cost.
