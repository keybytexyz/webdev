Prof. Somali Chaterji to serve as a panelist for the IPAI inaugural panel on "Shaping AI in the Physical World: Emerging Challenges and Opportunities," a discussion on how to address the challenges and opportunities presented by the science, engineering and implementation of AI systems into the physical world.
Please join us from noon to 1 p.m. ET on Tuesday, March 26 for "Shaping AI in the Physical World: Emerging Challenges and Opportunities," a discussion on how to address the challenges and opportunities presented by the science, engineering and implementation of AI systems into the physical world. The panel, featuring global leaders in fields of emerging technology, will be at the Bill and Shirley Rice Design Studio in the Material Sciences and Electrical Engineering Building at Purdue's West Lafayette campus and also streamed online. Registration is required and is available here. Lunch will provided for on-site attendees at 11:30 a.m. Organized by the Office of Research and the Institute for Physical AI, this Purdue-hosted event will bring together industry and research experts in AI to discuss the future of the field. The discussion will include representatives from Amazon, Argonne National Labs, Caterpillar, Purdue and Southwest Airlines. It is the first in a series supporting research on the intersection of AI and the physical world.

Please register today and join us for this inspiring event to shape the future of physical AI.
