KeyByte receives Trask award for further commercialization


KeyByte has received a Trask award from the Office of Technology Commercialization (OTC) of Purdue University for further development of its product portfolio and commercialization. This was awarded based on a written proposal and then an oral presentation by us as finalists. The presentation was evaluated by a set of judges from private companies in addition to OTC. Three winners were announced by Dr. Abhijit Karve, Director of Business Development at OTC in May.

This award will let us continue the momentum of development of the OptimusCloud product, the zero-configuration NoSQL database. In addition, we will be developing a serverless computing-based product. This award will let us keep the current developer team and add to it.
